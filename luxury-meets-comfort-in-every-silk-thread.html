FAQS

What makes silk a luxurious fabric?

Silk is considered luxurious due to its natural sheen, softness, and smooth texture. It drapes beautifully and has a rich history of being associated with elegance and high fashion.

How is premium silk different from regular silk?

Premium silk is typically made from the finest silkworms and undergoes stricter quality control during production. It features superior softness, durability, and a more vibrant sheen compared to regular silk.

Is silk comfortable to wear in all seasons?

Yes, silk is known for its breathability and temperature-regulating properties, making it comfortable to wear in both warm and cool weather. It helps keep you cool in summer and warm in winter.

How do I care for luxury silk products?

To care for luxury silk, it is best to hand wash it in cold water with a mild detergent, avoid harsh chemicals, and air dry it. Always check the care label for specific instructions.

Can silk be used in home dcor?

Absolutely! Silk can add elegance and sophistication to home dcor through items such as cushions and upholstery, enhancing the overall aesthetic of living spaces.
